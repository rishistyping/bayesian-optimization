Start with all possibilities

Begin with a uniform candidate set. Here we use numbers 1 to 100. The goal is to identify one hidden value by removing what evidence rules out.

Ask constraints

Each question is a binary constraint. A yes/no answer carves away possibilities and shrinks uncertainty.

Isolate by subtraction

Better updating is mostly elimination discipline: keep what survives all constraints, drop everything else, and avoid adding assumptions early.
